💡 Smart Spending

Money-Saving Tips

🌊 Climate Card 9-Day Pass
₩180,000 covers unlimited subway + bus for 9 days. Base fare ₩1,550/ride — it pays for itself at just 4 rides/day. Buy Day 2 morning at any subway station machine.
Saves ~₩100,000+
🏨 Hostel Swap for Seoul Nights
Substituting Airbnb nights (₩200,000/night) for hostel dorms (₩30,000/night) saves ~₩340,000 total across 2 nights.
Save up to ₩340,000
🎟️ Book Attractions on Klook
Everland (saves ~₩12,000), Seoul Sky, Sky Capsule, and Spa Land are all cheaper online. Always check Klook/Pelago first.
Save ~₩30,000+
🍺 Han River Picnic via Kakao Delivery
Order chicken via Baedal Minjok to a delivery zone near Yeouido. Mats from Daiso (₩1,000–2,000) or rental shops nearby. Much cheaper than a restaurant.
Save ~₩20,000
🍜 Convenience Stores Are Great
CU and GS25 have legitimately amazing food — samgyeopsal sandwiches, army stew, tteokbokki, fried chicken. Budget ₩5,000–7,000/meal.
~₩5,000–7,000/meal
🛒 Olive Young 1+1, 2+1 Deals
K-beauty deals are constant. Check the app or look for orange 1+1 / 2+1 tags on display stands. Myeongdong has the highest concentration of stores.
Up to 50% off
🏧 Avoid Airport Currency Exchange
Airport ATMs and exchange counters have terrible rates. Use Woori Bank or KEB Hana inside the city, or just use card and T-money everywhere.
Better FX rate
🥩 Majang Meat Market — Best Value
Premium hanwoo beef at wholesale prices. Buy downstairs, grill upstairs (₩6,000/pp grill fee). Go before 11AM for best cuts. This is a splurge that feels cheap.
Premium BBQ, great value
